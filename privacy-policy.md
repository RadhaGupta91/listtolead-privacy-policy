**Developer:** TechpileSolutions

## Overview
This Chrome extension is designed to assist with real estate listing automation.  
The extension does **not collect, store, transmit, or share** any personal or sensitive user data.

## Data Collection
The extension does **not**:
Collect personal information  
Store user data on external servers  
Track browsing history  
Use analytics or advertising trackers  
Share data with third parties
  

All actions performed by the extension occur **locally within the user's browser**.

## How the Extension Works
The extension interacts only with the active webpage (such as Realm | TREB or Facebook Marketplace) to automate posting tasks.  
No information is transmitted outside the user's device.

## User Control
Users may disable or remove the extension at any time.  
Because no data is stored, removing the extension permanently deletes all local settings.
